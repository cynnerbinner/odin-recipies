Wet Bread is the Best Bread!

Ingredients

  • 2 loaves good bread
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 celery stalks
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp sage and thyme mix

Steps

  1. Heat large saucepan on medium. Add butter, then saute onions and celery until softened.
  2. Add herbs and stir, cooking about 30 seconds.
  3. Add diced bread and chicken broth. Stir thoroughly.
  4. Cover with foil and bake in 350F oven for 30 mins.
