Roasted Root Vegetables

Because You Need Something Healthy!

Ingredients

  • Brussels sprouts
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Fennel bulb
  • Really, whatever is in the fridge
  • Olive oil
  • Maple syrup
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425F.
  2. Dice all the veggies up into half inch cubes.
  3. Toss with olive oil, maple syryp, thyme, salt and pepper.
  4. Spread evenly across baking sheet, making sure not to crowd.
  5. Bake uncovered for 25 - 30 mins.
